Donor

  • Any person who wishes to donate their old or new stuff like bagpacks, textbooks, laptops and other resource materials will have to login into the donor domain using email id and phone number where he/she will post the picture of the stuff they want to donate.
  • For every donated item purchased and successfully delivered to the student, the donor will get a certain reward points.
  • A certificate of appreciation for their contribution at the end of a year.
  • Contributor can wish to remain anonymous and can donate any number of items which are in useable condition.

Student

  • Student can browse through various products which are available (products that are collected from the donors) and they can place order.
  • To make sure products are supplied to all students we impose a limit of 2 products per week for each student.
  • Once the order is placed the delivery agent home delivers the product to the given address and if the student wishes to donate the item after their use, they can post it in the same website.
  • Students can also browse through various scholarship links provided in the website and apply for various govt. schemes

About us

Helpmate provides free books to everyone across every possible part of India. To guide donor's books to the reader's destiny is our business.

We aim to make India more powerful by sharing the gift of knowledge from those who supply it to those who need it. We want to be their first choice for donating and buying educational resources and gaining their trust. HELPMATE is an online platform was is created not only to just connect the Donors and Readers across India but also, be with them throughout the journey of all those reading years and help them with all available best resources.

We are on a mission to create an ecosystem where no Reader in the country is left to complete his/her education due to the affordability issue and unavailability of school supplies. We also believe in the reincarnation of supplies for the sustainability of products.


We want to assure :
"For the ones in need ,Help will always be delivered !!!"
